title: Sunset clock
description: A clock face whose colour drifts from dawn through dusk over a real Mumbai day.
date: 2026-05-08
tags: [color, time, css]
---

The face is one CSS gradient. The gradient stops are pinned to four times of day — sunrise, noon, sunset, midnight — and interpolated by the current local time. No JavaScript animation; the gradient just updates once a minute.

Less a tool than a slow ornament for a tab I leave open while I work.
